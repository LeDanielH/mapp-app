Discount management

(2.1)

Why my discount dissapeard from the list?

There are 3 possibilities why the disount is dissapeared from the list

  1. The discount is expired and only can be found in the expired list;
  2. The discount is deleted by merchants
  3. The discount is deleted by HCC
  • For a), please check the expired list
  • For b), please discuss whether the discount is deleted internally
  • For c). Deleted by HCC are because of the failed discount validation on operation side. Please make sure no sensitive or illegal information contains in discount and submit again
